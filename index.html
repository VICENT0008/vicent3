Welcome to Elite High School Entebbe.” What a joy to know that your daughter/son will experience a holistic education in a Christian high school! (Where they will be taught according to the truth). We are a unique educational Institution, offering a Uganda Curriculum provided by NCDC and examined by UNEB for both O and A-Level section. We are mission focused, integrating a clearly articulated biblical mission into practice and programme, hence our motto “Walk the Talk” Titus 2:7 It is my pleasure and honour to invite you to come and experience for yourself the loving and caring environment of Elite on the serene hills of Bwebajja- Entebbe road overlooking Lake Victoria.

A Centre of excellence that shapes exceptional individuals who will make a defining difference in our world.
